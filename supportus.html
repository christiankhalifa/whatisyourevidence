What it funds

Current projects.

A digital platform — clear, well-sourced, multilingual — designed to make the gospel and the comparative-religion case accessible at the tap of a screen.

02 / Book

An in-depth, primary-source-led study of the origins and influences behind the Qur’ānic text. Currently in writing.
